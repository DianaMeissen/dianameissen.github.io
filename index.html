Online education has many advantages that enhance business. But its essential drawback is a lack of face-to-face interaction. It leads to several problems in distance education, that business has to address.
The problems of distance education we focus on:
Lack of control during online training and testing
Lack of control may lead to fake learning and manipulation during test-taking, especially within mandatory and compliance training. It may result in business, compliance and life risks, big penalties and loss of reputation
Solve the problem
Lack of insight how to personalise program
In online education business gets to know only dry statistical data, such as how many trainees were enrolled, number of opened/ closed lessons, how many of them passed exams. We know nothing about how a particular person learned, how many efforts he/she spent, and how it influenced test results? Without this information no personalization of education program is possible
Solve the problem
Lack of data to measure efficiency of materials
Business doesn't have insightful information regarding the learning experience of the pool of employees. For example, which part of materials were learnt deeply and which were just skimmed. As a result, there is no data how to measure effectiveness of materials and how to optimise them
Solve the problem
